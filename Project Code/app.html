Energy Saving Tips (Energy_Saving Group)

Select an application from the usage list on the Dashboard to get application-specific tips, or view general tips below:

General Tips for Home:

  • Switch to LED bulbs for maximum light output at minimal wattage.
  • Unplug electronics (phantom loads) when not in use.
  • Set your Air Conditioner thermostat a few degrees higher to save a significant amount of energy.
  • Use washing machines and dishwashers only when they are fully loaded.
  • Consider using a programmable thermostat to optimize heating and cooling schedules.
  • Seal windows and doors to prevent air leaks and improve insulation.
  • Regularly maintain appliances to ensure they are running efficiently.
  • Use natural light during the day instead of electric lighting.
  • Consider energy-efficient appliances when replacing old ones.
  • Reduce water heating expenses by using less hot water and insulating your water heater.
